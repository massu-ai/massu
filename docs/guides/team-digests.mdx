# Team Digests

Receive automated weekly summaries of your team's AI development activity, delivered straight to your inbox.

## Requirements

- **Plan**: Cloud Team or higher
- **Email**: Verified email address on your team account

## What's Included

Each weekly digest contains:

- **Quality Score Trend** -- Current score with week-over-week direction (improving, stable, declining)
- **Session Activity** -- Number of sessions completed by the team during the week
- **Cost Breakdown** -- Total AI spend for the week across all team members
- **Risk Areas** -- Files and modules flagged with elevated risk scores
- **Top Prompts** -- Best-performing prompts from the team's prompt library

## Setting Up Digests

### 1. Navigate to Digest Settings

Go to **Dashboard > Team > Digest** to access the digest configuration page.

### 2. Configure Delivery

| Setting | Description | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Schedule | Day of week to send | Monday |
| Timezone | Delivery timezone | UTC |
| Recipients | Team members who receive the digest | All members |

### 3. Customize Sections

Toggle which sections appear in the digest:

- Quality score summary
- Session activity count
- Cost breakdown
- Risk area alerts
- Top prompts

## Opting In and Out

Each team member can individually opt in or out of digest emails from their notification preferences in **Dashboard > Settings > Notifications**.

## Digest History

Past digests are archived in the dashboard. Navigate to **Dashboard > Team > Digest** to view previous weeks' summaries with the same data that was emailed.

## Troubleshooting

### Not receiving digests?

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify your email address in account settings
3. Ensure you haven't opted out in notification preferences
4. Confirm your organization has an active Cloud Team or Enterprise plan
